About Us

The Krusty Krab is a legendary underwater restaurant that has been serving up delicious Krabby Patties and other ocean-inspired delicacies to residents and visitors of Bikini Bottom for many years. Founded by Mr. Eugene H. Krabs, the Krusty Krab has become an iconic establishment in the underwater world of SpongeBob SquarePants.
